Greentwix

Life is full of little surprises. — Pandora

Stormy summer night... A man walking down the street sees all things shaped and shaded unearthly, some of them are so vague that seem to have no nature. A rare passer-by resembles an ugly faceless humanoid.

Occasionally the man hears in the air a series of orders. He must go somewhere. He goes, yet scarcely knows the aim, and sees a stranger approaching... The new order - "touch him"... With an object suddenly appeared in his hand he slightly touches the stranger causing a quick metamorphosis. Another townsman has just become a zombie. The man goes further... Something eerie is bound to happen, and... A huge lightning dazzles us, then absolute blackness and a roll of thunder in a distance...

Incredible as it may seem, our hero has survived. He woke up in his bed and saw the usual ambience in daylight. A woman seated beside the bed. She told him what has happened: "You were struck by a lightning this night. The doctors are great deal puzzled that you were not killed or just injured. I live in the neighborhood, third house to the right. Stayed here to see to you till you're all right, so, suppose, I can go now."

So the game begins. The whole story takes place in the small town Greentwix where our hero lives. He is a photographer of the local newspaper Querty. Besides him we will meet some other characters in the town: beggar, policeman, barrow boy, yard-keeper etc. A lunatic sitting near the church would sometimes provide important knowledge in the form of apocalyptic warnings.

The town consists of a few dwelling houses, Querty editorial office, police station, church and the office of "Astindaz" brewer company. On the outskirts of the town one would find a town dump, electric distribution station and a small cemetery. Quite a complicated sewerage system is hidden beneath the ground. Only one road runs across the town and you can rarely see a car or truck passing by.

On the whole, the plot of the game is straight. It consists of some key events, first of them is a strike of lightning, all others the hero will inevitably go through. Nevertheless, the hero is free to investigate the area, communicate with other personages and fulfill intermediate tasks in any order. In other words, many various ways exist to achieve the same objects. In the course of the game the hero should solve a riddle of strange mutations the inhabitants of the town undergo, find out a way how to stop these mutations, and finally eliminate the source of this menace.
